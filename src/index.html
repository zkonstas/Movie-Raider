Welcome to Movie Raider!

Planning for a movie night?
Browse popular movies in theaters today on the home page, or find your own movies through the search bar. Add movies to your selection and find showtimes at a theater near you. Simply enter the zipcode and the time under Theater, and find the theaters that best fit your selection.

Filled with movie information!
Besides the release date, runtime, MPA rating, and synopsis, we also have reviews! Click on the collapsible "Review" to view Rotten Tomatoes and New York Times reviews. Once you've added a movie to the queue, you can still view this information by clicking on its icon.
